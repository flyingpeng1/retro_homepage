Hello, I'm Pengu!n

I am a "professional internet guy" and a student computer scientist at the University of Maryland. I have long been fascinated with the style of web 1.0 Geocities sites; the aesthetic is hard to explain but easy to identify. When I learned about Neocities, I decided to try my hand at reproducing that feel as a little side-project. If you would like to see all of my work or my resume, please leave a message in the guestbook or click through the about page to my social media. Any interaction is much appreciated, as being a creator internet guy, I must compete with millions of others for your attention.
